About the system

Here are some features that this system has.

format_list_bulleted
Forms

This system has a login, sign up and email form that connects to the database, using sequelize

outgoing_mail
Sending Emails

After sending an email, the user is redirected to the home page, he can visualize the email he just sent in the "Sent" page

alternate_email
Simple and objective

This system is simple, objective and minimalistic, does not send emails to other plataforms, like Gmail or Outlook
